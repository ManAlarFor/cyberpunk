How it all started

The very first Cyberpunk media even before the creation of the term were the american comic Judge Dredd and the japanese manga Akira, both showing a dystopic future and a society colapse. However, the start of this genre is almost always settled by some writters that wrote stories fulled of social critics, as William Gibson, with his book Neuromancer, as well as other authors like Bruce Sterling and John Shirley.
