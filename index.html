What cyberpunk is?

Cyberpunk is a genre that explores onto what posibilities a distopical and technological world could await.

From a robot revolution, collosal cities or even a world where its usual to see humans with loads of implants an bionic parts.

It is a genre filled up with amazing and horrifying stories about what the world could look like in the future.
